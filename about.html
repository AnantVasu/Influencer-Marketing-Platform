INFLUENCERX

It's astonishing how the Social media has transformed with the evolution of
Influencers and bloggers. The need for generating stupendous content is identified
and being practiced to reform social media and make it useful. For all the esteemed
promotions the talented influencers are prioritised. However, searching for a
perfect influencer for a brand or vice versa consumes a long time.

INFLUENCERX is therefore established to make this task simpler and
shorter for the brands and the bloggers by providing them a single platform.
The former and latter can register with INFLUENCERX and filter according to their
requirements to choose their desired party. The company first verifies the brand
or the blogger as soon as they register with it to create a transparent and trustworthy
community. It believes in providing the users with several opportunities for their growth.
